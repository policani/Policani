# Charter Generation Prompt

Generate an initiation-ready project charter using the evidence, intake responses, and review notes.

The charter must include:

- Header and authorization
- Purpose and business outcome
- Scope: in scope and out of scope
- Authorized deliverables
- Success measures
- Assumptions and constraints
- Risks, dependencies, and open decisions
- Stakeholders, roles, and governance
- Change-control model
- Milestone path from initiation to planning and execution
- Approvals

Do not reargue the business case. Reference it briefly as the authorization basis.
Use direct, plain language.
