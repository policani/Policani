# Charter Quality Review Prompt

Review the charter as a sponsor, delivery lead, finance partner, technology owner, and operations owner.

Score it against the rubric:

- Purpose and business outcome
- Authorization and ownership
- Scope boundaries
- Authorized deliverables
- Success measures
- Assumptions and constraints
- Risks and dependencies
- Governance and change control
- Milestone path

Return:

- Overall readiness score
- Fatal defects, if any
- Material gaps
- Recommended fixes
- Sponsor approval risks
- Final readiness recommendation
