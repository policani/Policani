# Operating Models

Operating models in this portfolio show how complex work can move through an organization with clearer ownership, cadence, decision logic, and evidence expectations.

They are public-safe examples of how to make the work easier to steer:

- Who owns the decision
- What evidence is required
- What constraints shape sequencing
- Which risks need executive attention
- Which commitments require follow-through

## What the artifacts demonstrate

The strongest through-line is governed decision support: turning scattered activity into a clearer system of record, review, escalation, and action.
