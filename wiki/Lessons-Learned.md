# Lessons Learned

The lessons-learned layer captures what tends to matter when complex work becomes hard to govern.

These are not employer case studies. They are generalized lessons from real operating environments, translated into public-safe language.

## Recurring lessons

- Activity is not the same as governed work.
- Intake quality shapes every later prioritization conversation.
- Readiness should be visible before a team commits to delivery confidence.
- Executive tradeoffs need evidence, options, and decision asks, not just status updates.
- AI can improve structure and review coverage, but humans must keep accountability for approvals, risk acceptance, funding, and commitments.
- Public examples need enough substance to show judgment without exposing confidential source material.
