# AI-Safe Workflow Design

The AI-related artifacts in this portfolio focus on human-governed workflow design.

They use AI for structure, synthesis, classification, drafting, review coverage, and repeatability. They do not present AI as the owner of approvals, funding, sequencing, risk acceptance, legal interpretation, compliance authority, or final commitments.

## What the artifacts demonstrate

- Business-value-first AI intake
- Proof requirements before tool reliance
- Human review and accountability boundaries
- Evidence registers and source separation
- Workflow artifacts that can be inspected and improved
- Public-safe examples that avoid confidential employer or client detail
