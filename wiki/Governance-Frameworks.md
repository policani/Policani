# Governance Frameworks

Governance frameworks in this portfolio show how ambiguous work becomes reviewable and governable.

They focus on:

- Intake and routing
- Ownership and sponsor clarity
- Prioritization and sequencing
- Readiness and evidence gates
- Decision rights and escalation paths
- Value realization and follow-through

## What the artifacts demonstrate

The public examples show how to separate active work from demand, demand from approval, approval from readiness, and readiness from value realization. That separation is often where better portfolio decisions begin.
