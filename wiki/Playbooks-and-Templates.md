# Playbooks and Templates

Playbooks and templates in this portfolio show repeatable ways to prepare governance conversations, decision artifacts, business cases, charters, review packs, readiness checks, and follow-up records.

They are designed to help a human lead do the work with better structure, not to replace judgment.

## What the artifacts demonstrate

- Source-backed drafting
- Clear assumptions and open decisions
- Risk, dependency, and readiness visibility
- Executive-facing summaries
- Human review and approval boundaries
- Reusable outputs that can survive real delivery pressure
