# Sample Prompt Pack

## 1. Start from a rough idea

I have a rough idea for a business case. Our field service teams are losing time because dispatch, parts availability, and technician scheduling are spread across multiple tools. Help me determine whether this is a real business case, what evidence is missing, and what questions I need to answer before drafting.

## 2. Challenge my framing

Before drafting, challenge my problem framing. Identify whether I am describing a symptom, a root cause, or a preferred solution. Ask no more than 8 questions needed to make this business case defensible.

## 3. Build from artifacts

Use the notes, CSVs, and project plan I uploaded to build a business case. Separate facts from assumptions, identify conflicts, and tell me which financial or operational evidence is too weak to support the recommendation.

## 4. Review for CFO scrutiny

Review this draft as a skeptical CFO. Focus on cost completeness, hard vs. soft benefits, ROI assumptions, sensitivity, opportunity cost, and what would need to be validated before approval.

## 5. Export final

Produce the final business case in Markdown, then provide an HTML-ready version and DOCX-ready version with clean headings and tables.
