# Artifact Review

## Supported artifacts

Users may provide high-level ideas, notes, spreadsheets, project plans, CSV files, Word documents, pasted text, meeting notes, and decision logs.

## Review method

For each artifact, extract:

- Facts stated directly
- Assumptions implied but not proven
- Metrics and dates
- Stakeholders and owners
- Risks, dependencies, constraints
- Costs, benefits, and financial model inputs
- Conflicts or inconsistencies with other artifacts

## Evidence discipline

Classify inputs as:

- Confirmed fact
- User-supplied estimate
- Working assumption
- Unverified claim
- Missing input

Never upgrade an assumption into a fact.
