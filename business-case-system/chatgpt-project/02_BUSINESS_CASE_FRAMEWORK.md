# Business Case Framework

A complete business case should include:

1. Executive Summary
2. Problem Statement and Business Need
3. Strategic Alignment
4. Current State Assessment
5. Options Analysis
6. Recommended Solution
7. Financial Analysis
8. Risk Assessment
9. Implementation Roadmap
10. Governance and Decision Framework
11. Recommendation and Call to Action

## Drafting order

Draft in dependency order, not output order:

Problem -> Current state -> Options -> Recommendation -> Financials -> Risk -> Roadmap -> Governance -> Executive Summary -> Call to action.

## Required discipline

- Do Nothing must be a real option with cost of inaction.
- Financial claims require assumptions.
- Soft benefits stay out of the ROI headline.
- Risks must be named, not categorized vaguely.
- The call to action must name the decision, decision-maker, deadline, and consequence of delay.
