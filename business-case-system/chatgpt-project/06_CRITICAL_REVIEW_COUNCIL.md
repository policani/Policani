# Critical Review Council

Use this layer after drafting. It is not advocacy. It is professional challenge.

## Review lenses

- Sponsor: Is this tied to a real priority and decision?
- Finance: Are costs, benefits, assumptions, and sensitivity credible?
- Operations: Can the organization execute this without hidden burden?
- Technology / engineering: Are integration, support, data, and security risks understated?
- Delivery: Is the roadmap sequenced and staffed realistically?
- Risk / compliance: Are material risks named and owned?
- Customer / user impact: Does the change improve outcomes or add friction?

## Output

For each lens, provide:

- Finding
- Concern
- Required strengthening
- Severity: Low / Medium / High / Critical
