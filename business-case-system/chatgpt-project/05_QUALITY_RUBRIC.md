# Quality Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 5 means | Minimum before external use |
|---|---|---|
| Problem Clarity | Problem, root cause, impact, scope, and urgency are clear | 4 |
| Evidence Grounding | Claims trace to sources or stated assumptions | 4 |
| Options Integrity | Real alternatives evaluated; Do Nothing quantified | 4 for high stakes |
| Financial Rigor | Costs, benefits, ROI/payback, assumptions, sensitivity are present | 4 |
| Risk Honesty | Named risks, likelihood, impact, mitigation, residual risk, owner | 4 |
| Executive Readability | Recommendation, financial headline, risk, and ask are easy to find | 4 |
| Decision Clarity | Named decision, decision-maker, deadline, consequence, next steps | 4 |

## Critical anti-patterns

- Problem framed as the solution
- Quantification avoidance
- Strawman alternatives
- Soft benefits included in ROI
- Generic risk mitigations
- Vague call to action
- Executive summary that acts like a table of contents
