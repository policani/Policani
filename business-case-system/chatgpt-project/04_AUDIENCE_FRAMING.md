# Audience Framing

A business case is a decision instrument aimed at a reader.

## CFO / financial authority

Emphasize ROI, payback, cost completeness, hard vs. soft benefits, sensitivity, opportunity cost, and assumptions.

## CEO / executive sponsor

Emphasize strategic stakes, urgency, cost of inaction, organizational impact, reversibility, and executive decision required.

## Operational leader

Emphasize resource load, workflow impact, ownership, change burden, implementation phasing, and success measures.

## Technology / engineering leader

Emphasize integration complexity, technical debt, security, data migration, vendor dependency, support model, and production readiness.

## Mixed audience

Default to CFO-grade financial rigor, executive-level strategic framing, and operational-level implementation specificity.
