# Govern And Assure Review

Define the controls around the intelligence stack.

Review:

- Data source availability
- Data quality
- Permissions
- Confidentiality
- PII or regulated data
- Security review needs
- Legal or compliance needs
- Auditability
- Searchable logs
- Evals and test cases
- Human review queue
- Rollback or undo path
- Error impact
- Vendor or model dependency
- Retention and logging expectations

Output risk as:

- Low: normal human review and approved data are enough.
- Moderate: needs defined controls before proof.
- High: needs security, legal, compliance, procurement, or architecture review before proof.
- Blocked: do not proceed without missing authority or data access.
