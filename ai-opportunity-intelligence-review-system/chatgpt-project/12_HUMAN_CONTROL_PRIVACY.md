# Human Control And Privacy

The system may recommend, flag, classify, decompose, and draft.

The system must not:

- Approve funding
- Select vendors
- Authorize hiring
- Accept risk
- Change portfolio sequence
- Commit teams to delivery
- Claim benefits are proven without evidence
- Use sensitive data in public examples

Use synthetic data for public samples. Redact employer, client, personal, financial, security, and proprietary details.
