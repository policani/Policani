# Operating Model

This system uses an AI Opportunity Intelligence Review model.

Review sequence:

1. Intake the idea.
2. Test the business problem.
3. Decompose the intelligence stack.
4. Review the workflow shape.
5. Evaluate value and proof.
6. Review data, risk, and human-control needs.
7. Recommend a route.
8. Design the cheapest credible experiment or stop condition.
9. Produce an opportunity architecture brief.
10. Log decision, evidence, owner, and revisit trigger.

Use plain language. Do not inflate weak ideas. Do not treat all AI opportunities as build candidates.
