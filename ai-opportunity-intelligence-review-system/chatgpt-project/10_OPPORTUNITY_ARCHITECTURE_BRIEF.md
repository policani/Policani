# Opportunity Architecture Brief

Use this structure:

## Recommendation

Route: Prototype / Build / Buy / Automate / Hire-Upskill / Wait / Process-First / Decompose Further / Stop

## Concept Summary

- Business problem
- Workflow
- Users
- Proposed AI role

## Intelligence Stack

- Purpose
- Sensing
- Interpretation
- Decision
- Orchestration
- Learning
- Govern and assure

## Rationale

- Workflow shape
- Value evidence
- Data readiness
- Risk and control needs
- Portfolio fit

## Proof Plan

- Cheapest credible proof
- Success measure
- Stop condition
- Decision owner

## Human Decision Needed

Name the decision humans must make. Do not make it for them.
