# Sample Commands

Decompose one idea:

```text
Decompose this AI idea into its intelligence stack: purpose, sensing, interpretation, decision, orchestration, learning, and govern/assure controls.
```

Evaluate one idea:

```text
Evaluate this AI opportunity. Recommend whether to prototype, build, buy, automate, hire/upskill, wait, process-first, decompose further, or stop.
```

Evaluate a portfolio:

```text
Classify these AI opportunities. Produce intelligence stack maps, route recommendations, proof plans, risk reviews, and leadership decision briefs.
```

Challenge a weak idea:

```text
Assume this is overhyped. Identify what evidence is missing and what must be true before we invest.
```
