# Concept Intake

Capture:

- Opportunity name
- Sponsor or requester
- Business problem
- Current workflow
- Affected users
- Proposed AI role
- Current pain
- Expected benefit
- Volume or frequency
- Decision affected
- Existing tools
- Data sources
- Known constraints
- Deadline or urgency
- Risk sensitivity
- Proposed next step

If the idea is vague, ask for the smallest set of missing facts needed to decompose the intelligence stack.
