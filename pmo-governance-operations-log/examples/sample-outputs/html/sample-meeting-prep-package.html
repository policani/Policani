Sample Meeting Prep Package

Purpose

Resolve active blockers and confirm near-term governance follow-through.

Agenda

  1. Customer Portal / Data Platform dependency
  2. Regulatory validation date
  3. Finance phase 2 approval
  4. Open action review

Facilitator Notes

  • Keep status updates short.
  • Ask for decision, owner, date, and next step on each open item.
  • Do not leave the dependency topic without confirming escalation path.
