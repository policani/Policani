Meeting Operations Guide

Before the meeting

  • Collect updates.
  • Review prior actions.
  • Identify decisions and escalations.
  • Prepare agenda and facilitator notes.

During the meeting

  • Keep discussion on decisions, blockers, and tradeoffs.
  • Capture owners and due dates.
  • Separate discussion from decision.

After the meeting

  • Update decision and action logs.
  • Send stakeholder follow-ups.
  • Schedule follow-up meetings.
  • Carry forward unresolved items.
