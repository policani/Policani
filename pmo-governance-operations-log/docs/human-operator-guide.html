Human Operator Guide

The PMO Governance Operations Log supports the person who runs the governance rhythm. It helps convert rough notes and operational noise into practical governance artifacts.

Primary use cases

  • Prepare governance meetings.
  • Summarize the week.
  • Process rough meeting notes.
  • Identify overdue follow-ups.
  • Draft escalation briefs for executive air support.
  • Recommend project plan updates for human confirmation.

Operating rule

The tool supports the operator. It does not own decisions, commitments, approvals, risk acceptance, or funding choices.
