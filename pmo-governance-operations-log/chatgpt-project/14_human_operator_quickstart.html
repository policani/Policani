Human Operator Quickstart

Use this runtime as a PMO governance worklog assistant. Capture rough notes during the week, then ask the agent to convert them into governance summaries, follow-up lists, decision logs, escalation briefs, and next-meeting prep.

Basic cycle

  1. Capture rough notes as they happen.
  2. Drop the notes into ChatGPT.
  3. Ask for classification and weekly governance outputs.
  4. Review and correct the outputs.
  5. Carry forward unresolved items to the next cycle.

Best prompt

Review this week's PMO operations log and produce an executive summary, decisions needed, escalation candidates, overdue actions, stakeholder follow-ups, risks/dependencies, project plan update recommendations, and next-meeting prep.
