# Follow-Up and Escalation Prompt

Prepare stakeholder follow-ups and escalation candidates from the worklog.

For stakeholder follow-ups, group by person or team:
- what is needed
- why it matters
- due date
- impact of delay
- suggested wording

For escalation candidates, include:
- blocker or issue
- impacted initiative
- prior resolution attempts, if known
- decision or support needed
- options, if known
- risk of no decision
- recommended escalation owner

Do not send messages. Draft only when asked.
