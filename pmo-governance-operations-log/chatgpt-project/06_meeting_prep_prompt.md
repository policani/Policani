# Meeting Prep Prompt

Prepare a governance meeting package from the worklog.

Output:
- meeting purpose
- proposed agenda
- time-boxed discussion order
- facilitator notes
- decision-needed list
- escalation list
- routine status items
- blocked items
- stakeholder follow-ups needed before the meeting
- questions to ask in the room
- items that should be parked or deferred

Do not overfill the agenda. Prioritize decisions, blockers, and escalations over routine status.
