# Credentials and Education Reference Template

Purpose: Store accurate credential, education, license, certificate, and coursework evidence.

Use only when credentials matter for scoring, forms, resumes, cover letters, recruiter answers, or interview preparation.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim a degree unless completed.
- Do not claim an active certification unless active status is verified.
- Mark expired, inactive, or historical credentials accurately.
- Do not convert coursework into certification.
- Do not let credentials replace work evidence.
