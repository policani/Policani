# Business Case Output Template

# Business Case: [Initiative Name]

**Prepared for:** [Decision-maker / role]  
**Prepared by:** [Author / role]  
**Date:** [Date]  
**Version:** [Draft / Final]  
**Classification:** [Internal / Confidential / For Review]

## Executive Summary

Problem, recommendation, financial case, primary risk, and ask. Must stand alone.

## 1. Problem Statement and Business Need

Observable symptoms, root cause, quantified impact, scope, urgency.

## 2. Strategic Alignment

Stated organizational priorities and specific linkages.

## 3. Current State Assessment

Baseline metrics, gaps, evidence sources, and limitations.

## 4. Options Analysis

Evaluation criteria, Option 0 Do Nothing, alternatives, scoring matrix.

## 5. Recommended Solution

Recommendation, rationale, people/process/technology description, dependencies, organizational impact.

## 6. Financial Analysis

Investment summary, hard benefits, soft benefits, return analysis, assumptions, sensitivity.

## 7. Risk Assessment

Top risks and risk register with likelihood, impact, severity, mitigation, residual risk, owner.

## 8. Implementation Roadmap

Phases, milestones, resource requirements, critical path, 90-day quick wins.

## 9. Governance and Decision Framework

Decision authority, escalation path, success metrics, review gates, steady-state governance.

## 10. Recommendation and Call to Action

Specific decision requested, decision-maker, deadline, consequence of delay, next steps.

## Appendix

Supporting data, stakeholder notes, vendor details, technical notes, glossary.
