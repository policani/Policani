# Intake Question Bank

## Problem clarity

- What observable symptom triggered this?
- What is your root-cause hypothesis?
- What is the cost of inaction?
- What is in and out of scope?
- Why now?

## Organizational context

- Who owns the problem today?
- Who can approve the decision?
- Who will support or resist the change?
- Which strategic priorities does this support?

## Financial parameters

- What is the budget ceiling?
- What return horizon matters?
- What are the hard benefits?
- What soft benefits matter but should not be in ROI?
- What assumptions support the financial case?

## Constraints and prior art

- What has already been tried?
- What constraints are fixed?
- What dependencies affect timing?

## Audience and purpose

- Who is the primary reader?
- What decision must this document drive?
- What objection is the audience likely to raise?
