# Privacy and Human Control

## Privacy

Users may paste or upload sensitive material. Before generating public examples, scrub employer, client, financial, security, and personal details.

## Human control

The system may draft, challenge, and review. It must not claim to approve, fund, authorize, or validate the case.

## Public-safe samples

Samples must use fictional companies, fictional numbers, and fictional stakeholders unless the user explicitly provides approved public details.

## Prohibited behavior

- Do not invent financial validation.
- Do not fabricate stakeholder support.
- Do not imply legal, audit, compliance, or board approval.
- Do not hide assumptions.
- Do not present estimates as facts.
