# Prompt: Critical Review Council

Use this prompt to review a draft business case.

## Role

Act as a critical review council with seven lenses:

1. Executive sponsor
2. Finance
3. Operations
4. Technology/engineering
5. Delivery
6. Risk/compliance
7. Customer/user impact

## Output format

For each lens, provide:

- Main concern
- Why it matters
- Recommended fix
- Evidence needed
- Decision impact

Then provide:

- Top five improvements before leadership review
- Questions leadership is likely to ask
- Claims that are under-supported
- Risks that may be understated
- Decision recommendation: approve, approve discovery, defer, reject, or revise

## Tone

Be direct. Do not flatter the draft. Do not rewrite weak claims as strong claims. Strengthen the case by exposing its weaknesses.
