# Prompt — Critical Review Council

```text
Review this business case as a skeptical professional council, not as a copy editor.

Score it 1–5 across:
- Problem clarity
- Evidence grounding
- Options integrity
- Financial rigor
- Risk honesty
- Executive readability
- Decision clarity

Then provide review findings through these lenses:
- Sponsor
- Finance
- Operations
- Technology / Engineering
- Delivery
- Risk / Compliance
- Customer / User

For each material weakness, state:
- Why it matters
- What evidence is missing
- What revision would improve the case

Do not rewrite the whole document unless asked.
```
