# Prompt — Audience Reframe

```text
Reframe this business case for the following primary audience: [CFO / CEO / operational leader / technology leader / interview panel / multi-audience].

Do not change the facts. Change only emphasis, sequencing, and language.

For the selected audience:
- Strengthen the executive summary
- Emphasize the objections that audience is most likely to raise
- Adjust the financial, operational, technology, or strategic emphasis accordingly
- Keep the decision request explicit
- Preserve evidence boundaries and assumptions
```
