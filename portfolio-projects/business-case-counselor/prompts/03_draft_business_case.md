# Prompt — Draft Business Case

```text
Using the confirmed situation brief and evidence inventory, draft the business case in the standard structure.

Draft internally in this order:
1. Problem statement
2. Current state
3. Root cause
4. Strategic alignment
5. Options analysis
6. Recommendation
7. Financial analysis
8. Risk assessment
9. Implementation roadmap
10. Governance
11. Executive summary
12. Call to action

Output in business case order. Include Do Nothing as Option 0. Separate hard benefits from soft benefits. Include sensitivity analysis if financial assumptions are material. Name risks specifically and assign owners.

Use plain executive-readable language. Do not use consulting filler or unsupported claims.
```
