# Prompt: DOCX Formatting Guardrails

Use this when generating or reviewing a Word version of the business case.

## Formatting requirements

- Use Heading 1 for major sections.
- Use Heading 2 for subsections.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Use tables for options, costs, benefits, risks, assumptions, and decisions.
- Make the executive summary readable on its own.
- Avoid dense walls of text.
- Put the decision request near the front.
- Include an evidence appendix if artifacts were provided.

## Quality checks

Before final delivery, verify:

- All major headings are present.
- The document has no placeholder text left unresolved.
- Tables are readable.
- The recommendation is clear.
- Risks and assumptions are not buried.
- The “do nothing” option is included.
- The document can be used as an input to project chartering and portfolio governance.
