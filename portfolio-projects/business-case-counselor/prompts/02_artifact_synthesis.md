# Prompt — Artifact Synthesis

```text
Review the provided notes, spreadsheets, project plans, CSV files, Word documents, and other source artifacts.

Extract only decision-useful evidence:
- Current-state metrics
- Cost or time impact
- Stakeholder concerns
- Prior attempts
- Constraints
- Dependencies
- Benefits and assumptions
- Risks
- Open questions

Separate facts from assumptions. Do not invent missing data. Where evidence is directional, label it as directional. Where source quality is weak, say so.

Return:
1. Evidence inventory
2. Confirmed facts
3. Working assumptions
4. Material gaps
5. Evidence that should influence the recommendation
```
