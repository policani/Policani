# Contributing

Contributions should improve decision quality, not just add more templates.

Good contributions include:

- Stronger interview questions
- Better maturity scoring criteria
- Artifact ingestion improvements
- Cleaner DOCX formatting
- Better examples
- Clearer review-council counsel

Avoid:

- Inflated ROI language
- Unsupported benefit claims
- Overly complex process models
- Tool-specific lock-in
- Jargon-heavy templates
