# Manifest

This package includes:

- Local Python CLI for workspace setup, maturity review, and Markdown/DOCX/HTML output generation.
- Agent-readable operating instructions in `AGENTS.md`.
- Business case framework, intake method, audience framing, quality rubric, and review council documentation.
- Prompt pack for adaptive intake, artifact synthesis, drafting, critical review, audience reframing, and final export.
- Templates for business case intake, output structure, costs/benefits, risks/assumptions/constraints, and decisions.
- Fictional sample evidence and generated outputs.

All sample data is fictional and intended for demonstration only.
