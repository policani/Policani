# Critical Review Council

The Critical Review Council is a set of professional challenge lenses applied to the business case.

## Sponsor lens

Tests strategic relevance, timing, executive clarity, and organizational priority.

## Finance lens

Tests hard benefits, cost completeness, ROI/payback, assumptions, and sensitivity.

## Operations lens

Tests feasibility, resource load, change burden, ownership, and operational disruption.

## Technology / Engineering lens

Tests integration, data, architecture, security, migration, vendor dependency, and support model.

## Delivery lens

Tests phasing, milestones, dependencies, critical path, and governance gates.

## Risk / Compliance lens

Tests named risks, controls, compliance exposure, auditability, and residual risk.

## Customer / User lens

Tests whether the proposed change improves the experience of the people affected rather than merely adding process overhead.
