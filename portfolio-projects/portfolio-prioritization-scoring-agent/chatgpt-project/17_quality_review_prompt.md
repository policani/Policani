# Prompt Pack 05 - Quality Review and Self-Critique

## Use when

Use this prompt before sharing a portfolio scorecard or decision-support brief with governance leaders.

## Prompt

Review the portfolio scoring model and outputs as a skeptical senior portfolio manager.

Assess:

1. Criteria clarity
2. Weight balance
3. Mandatory-work treatment
4. Evidence quality
5. Score confidence
6. Missing sponsor, owner, or decision authority
7. Cost and capacity realism
8. Benefit clarity
9. Risk and dependency visibility
10. Decision readiness
11. Potential stakeholder objections
12. Recommended fixes before executive review

Produce:

- Overall readiness rating: Ready, Ready with caveats, or Not ready
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Material risks
- Required corrections
- Optional improvements
- Decision-board questions

Do not flatter the work. Be candid and specific.
