# Prompt Pack 04 - Executive Decision-Support Brief

## Use when

Use this prompt after scoring and quality review, when preparing a portfolio board packet or executive summary.

## Prompt

Create an executive-ready portfolio decision-support brief.

Audience: portfolio board, executive sponsors, finance, technology, operations, and delivery leaders.

Tone: direct, plain-language, decision-focused, no hype, no false precision.

Include:

1. Executive summary
2. Portfolio context and assumptions
3. Scoring model and weights
4. Top ranked initiatives
5. Mandatory commitments
6. Discretionary trade-offs
7. Budget and capacity pressure
8. Risk and dependency view
9. Missing-data and governance flags
10. Recommended decision options
11. Human decisions required
12. Decision log appendix

Keep the distinction clear:

- The agent provides scoring and structured advice.
- Human leaders make funding, sequencing, and commitment decisions.
