Post Meeting Closeout Guide

Closeout is where governance becomes operational. The meeting is not complete until decisions, actions, owners, dates, unresolved topics, and carry-forward items are captured.

Closeout records

  • Decision log updates.
  • Action register updates.
  • Follow-up meetings to schedule.
  • Open questions.
  • Unresolved ownership gaps.
  • Next-cycle worklog seed.

Closeout test

A person who missed the meeting should be able to see what changed, who owes what, when it is due, and what still needs a decision.

Portfolio Governance Operations Log — synthetic, public-safe guidance. Human owners retain decision authority.
