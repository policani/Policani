Stakeholder Follow Up Guide

Stakeholder follow-up is not nagging. It is the operating discipline that keeps governance from losing signal between meetings.

Priority order

  1. Items blocking other teams.
  2. Decision inputs due before executive review.
  3. Overdue actions.
  4. Missing sponsor updates.
  5. Weak status that may mislead leadership.

Message pattern

Hi Jordan — I’m preparing the Thursday governance review. I need the current mitigation plan for the Field Operations adoption risk, including owner and next checkpoint. Please send by Wednesday 2:00 PM so I can keep the summary accurate.

What to avoid

  • Do not imply authority you do not have.
  • Do not threaten escalation prematurely.
  • Do not accept vague “on track” status when the milestone is at risk.
Portfolio Governance Operations Log — synthetic, public-safe guidance. Human owners retain decision authority.
