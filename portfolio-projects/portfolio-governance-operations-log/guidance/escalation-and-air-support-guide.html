Escalation And Air Support Guide

An executive air-support brief should clarify the problem, impact, prior resolution attempts, and the specific help needed. It should not dramatize the issue.

Escalation threshold

Escalate whenDo not escalate when
A dependency blocks committed work and peer resolution has stalled.The team has not attempted normal resolution.
A sponsor decision is needed to trade scope, time, cost, or quality.The issue is merely inconvenient.
Competing priorities require executive alignment.The ask is unclear.

Brief structure

  • Situation
  • Impact
  • Prior attempts
  • Options
  • Risk of no action
  • Requested executive action
