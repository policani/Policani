Meeting Preparation And Facilitation Guide

Governance meetings should not be passive status narration. The PMO facilitator should protect the time for decisions, blockers, ownership, and follow-through.

Preparation checklist

  • Confirm the decision-needed list.
  • Identify stale or unsupported green status.
  • Confirm overdue actions and missing updates.
  • Prepare escalation asks in plain language.
  • Draft stakeholder-specific questions before the meeting.

Facilitation pattern

SegmentFacilitator move
OpeningState the goal: decisions, blockers, and follow-through.
Routine statusHandle by exception only.
Decision itemsAsk for options, tradeoffs, and decision owner.
EscalationsClarify the executive ask and timing.
CloseoutRestate actions, owners, due dates, and unresolved items.
Portfolio Governance Operations Log — synthetic, public-safe guidance. Human owners retain decision authority.
