Artifact Ingestion And Privacy Guide

PMO notes often contain sensitive business context. Treat every source artifact as potentially confidential until reviewed.

Safe ingestion practices

  • Use synthetic data in public repositories.
  • Redact customer names, employee names, financials, security details, legal matters, and proprietary plans.
  • Do not paste credentials, incident reports, contracts, or private HR content into unapproved systems.
  • Store only what is needed for governance output.
