# Worklog Signal Quality Rubric

Use this rubric to review generated outputs. Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 1 — Weak | 3 — Adequate | 5 — Strong |
|---|---|---|---|
| Signal classification | Categories are missing or generic. | Most notes are classified. | Notes are classified with useful primary category, tags, urgency, and missing fields. |
| Missing information | Missing owners or dates are ignored. | Some missing fields are flagged. | Missing owners, dates, options, mitigation, evidence, and asks are clearly surfaced. |
| Actionability | Output is descriptive only. | Some next steps are listed. | Each actionable signal has a clear follow-up recommendation. |
