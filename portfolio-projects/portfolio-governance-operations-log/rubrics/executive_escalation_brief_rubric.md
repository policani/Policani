# Executive Escalation Brief Rubric

Use this rubric to review generated outputs. Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 1 — Weak | 3 — Adequate | 5 — Strong |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clarity of ask | Ask is vague or missing. | Ask is present but broad. | Specific executive support needed is clear. |
| Impact framing | Impact is exaggerated or unsupported. | Impact is plausible but thin. | Impact is factual, concise, and tied to affected initiatives. |
| Options and tradeoffs | No options. | Some options. | Options and risk of no action are clear without implying a decision. |
