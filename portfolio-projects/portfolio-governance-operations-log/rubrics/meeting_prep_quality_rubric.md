# Meeting Prep Quality Rubric

Use this rubric to review generated outputs. Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 1 — Weak | 3 — Adequate | 5 — Strong |
|---|---|---|---|
| Focus | Agenda is status-heavy. | Agenda includes some decisions and blockers. | Agenda protects time for decisions, blockers, ownership, and closeout. |
| Facilitation support | No useful prompts. | Basic prompts included. | PMO-ready transitions, challenge questions, and closeout language are included. |
| Time discipline | No timeboxes. | Some timeboxes. | Clear 60-minute plan with outcome per segment. |
