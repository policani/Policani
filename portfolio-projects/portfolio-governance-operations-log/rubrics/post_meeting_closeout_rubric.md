# Post-Meeting Closeout Rubric

Use this rubric to review generated outputs. Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

| Dimension | 1 — Weak | 3 — Adequate | 5 — Strong |
|---|---|---|---|
| Decision capture | Implied decisions are treated as approved. | Decisions are listed but some are unclear. | Only explicit decisions are logged; candidates are flagged. |
| Action quality | Actions lack owner or due date. | Most actions have owner/date. | Actions, owners, due dates, source, and follow-up are clear. |
| Carry-forward | Open items disappear. | Some open items carry forward. | Unresolved items seed the next governance cycle. |
