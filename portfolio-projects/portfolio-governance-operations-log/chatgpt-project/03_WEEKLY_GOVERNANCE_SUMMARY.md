# Weekly Governance Summary

## Purpose

Convert the classified worklog into an executive-readable weekly portfolio governance summary.

## Required sections

1. Executive summary
2. Top governance concerns
3. Decisions needed
4. Escalations and air-support candidates
5. Overdue or blocked actions
6. Missing updates and weak signals
7. Schedule and dependency changes
8. Project-plan / RAID / log updates to confirm
9. Carry-forward items
10. Recommended focus for next governance meeting

## Writing rules

- Lead with material governance signals, not chronological note history.
- Separate routine status from decisions, blockers, risks, and follow-up.
- Keep executive language factual and concise.
- State uncertainty plainly.
- Do not manufacture urgency.
- Do not imply decisions or approvals.

## Output length

Default to 1-2 pages in Markdown unless the user asks for more detail.

## Human-control statement

Any recommendation affecting scope, schedule, sequencing, funding, ownership, escalation, or risk acceptance requires human confirmation.
