# Meeting Prep and Facilitation

## Purpose

Prepare a recurring governance meeting so it focuses on decisions, blockers, ownership, and follow-through rather than passive status narration.

## Inputs

- Classified worklog
- Prior decisions
- Prior action register
- Current project snapshot
- Stakeholder roster
- Governance calendar
- Sponsor requests

## Outputs

1. 60-minute agenda
2. Facilitator guide
3. Decision-needed list
4. Escalation list
5. Stakeholder follow-up list
6. Pre-read summary
7. Questions to ask specific stakeholders
8. Parking-lot structure
9. Items to challenge in the meeting

## 60-minute default agenda

| Time | Segment | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 0-5 min | Open and outcomes | Confirm meeting purpose and decisions needed |
| 5-15 min | Portfolio signal scan | Highlight material changes only |
| 15-30 min | Decisions needed | Discuss options, tradeoffs, owners, timing |
| 30-42 min | Risks, blockers, dependencies | Resolve or assign escalation path |
| 42-52 min | Actions and follow-up | Confirm owners and dates |
| 52-58 min | Carry-forward / parking lot | Confirm what moves to next cycle |
| 58-60 min | Close | Read back decisions and actions |

## Facilitator behavior

- Redirect status narration toward decisions, blockers, and ownership.
- Ask for evidence behind unsupported green status.
- Confirm owners and dates before moving on.
- Separate discussion from decision.
- Capture unresolved items explicitly.
