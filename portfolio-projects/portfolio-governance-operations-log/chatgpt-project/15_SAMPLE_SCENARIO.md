# Sample Scenario

Use this fictional scenario to test the project.

## Initiatives

1. Regulatory Reporting Remediation — compliance-oriented; sponsor wants deadline confidence; some updates are vague; one decision is needed on scope deferral.
2. Customer Portal Modernization — customer-experience and growth-oriented; blocked by Data Platform readiness; needs executive air support to resolve prioritization conflict.
3. Field Operations Workflow Automation — operational-efficiency-oriented; has change-management risk and overdue stakeholder updates; one action owner has missed two updates.
4. Data Platform Stabilization — platform-foundation / technical debt-oriented; multiple teams depend on it; owner reports green but evidence is weak.

## Sample rough worklog notes

1. Mon: Regulatory Reporting owner says “green” but no milestone evidence provided.
2. Mon: Sponsor asked whether compliance deadline can still be met.
3. Mon: Regulatory team may defer lower-priority audit fields but options are unclear.
4. Tue: Customer Portal blocked by Data Platform API readiness.
5. Tue: Data Platform owner says stabilization is on track but dashboard has not changed in two weeks.
6. Tue: Portal team requests executive help resolving platform priority conflict.
7. Tue: Field Ops training plan still missing from change lead.
8. Wed: Governance session moved from Thursday to Friday due to sponsor conflict.
9. Wed: Action from prior meeting to confirm Field Ops pilot sites is overdue.
10. Wed: Data Platform defect burn-down date may move from May 31 to June 7.
11. Wed: Regulatory risk logged but no mitigation owner listed.
12. Thu: Follow-up meeting needed between Portal, Data Platform, and Architecture.
13. Thu: Field Ops stakeholder missed second update.
14. Thu: Customer Portal launch-readiness review may slip if API readiness not resolved.
15. Thu: Carry-forward from last meeting: confirm who owns cross-team dependency decisions.
16. Fri: Sponsor asks for concise decision brief before next governance meeting.

## Expected signals

- missing status update
- stale green status
- blocked dependency
- executive air-support need
- follow-up meeting to schedule
- rescheduled governance session
- overdue action
- risk with missing mitigation
- decision request with unclear options
- project-plan date change
- sponsor request
- stakeholder follow-up
- carry-forward item
