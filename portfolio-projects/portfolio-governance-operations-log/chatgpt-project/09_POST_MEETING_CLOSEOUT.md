# Post-Meeting Closeout

## Purpose

Turn meeting outcomes into follow-through records and seed the next governance cycle.

## Required sections

1. Meeting summary
2. Decisions confirmed
3. Decision log updates
4. Action register updates
5. Follow-up meetings to schedule
6. Risks / issues / blockers updated
7. Dependencies updated
8. Project-plan updates to confirm
9. Unresolved ownership gaps
10. Open questions
11. Carry-forward items
12. Next-cycle worklog seed

## Closeout rules

- Confirm every decision has decision owner, date, and scope.
- Confirm every action has owner and due date.
- Mark unclear decisions as unresolved.
- Carry forward unresolved blockers, dependencies, and missing updates.
- Do not let parking-lot topics disappear.
- Use `Needs confirmation` for anything implied but not explicit.
