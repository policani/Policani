# Quality Rubrics

## Worklog classification rubric

- Raw note preserved
- Initiative identified or marked missing
- Primary category assigned
- Supporting flags assigned
- Owner and due date captured or marked missing
- Action needed is specific
- Human confirmation is explicit

## Meeting prep rubric

- Agenda is timeboxed
- Decisions are separated from status
- Risks, blockers, dependencies, and escalations are visible
- Facilitator prompts are practical
- Stakeholder questions are specific
- Parking lot and carry-forward are included

## Executive brief rubric

- Issue is concise
- Business and timeline impact are factual
- Prior resolution attempts are listed
- Options are clear or marked missing
- Executive ask is specific
- No exaggerated urgency

## Closeout rubric

- Decisions are confirmed or marked unresolved
- Actions have owners and due dates or missing fields
- Follow-up meetings are listed
- Plan/log/register updates are identified
- Carry-forward items seed the next cycle

## Human-control rubric

- No implied approvals
- No invented facts
- Recommendations marked as recommendations
- Authority remains with humans
