# Facilitator Guide

## Opening

State the meeting purpose and orient discussion around decisions, blockers, ownership, and follow-through.

## Transition prompts

- “Is this routine status, or does it require a decision?”
- “Who owns the next action, and by when?”
- “What evidence supports the green status?”
- “What executive action is needed, specifically?”
- “What carries forward if we cannot resolve this today?”

## Closeout

Restate decisions, actions, due dates, follow-up meetings, unresolved items, and next-cycle seed.
