# Governance Meeting Agenda

**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Audience:** [Sponsors, initiative owners, PMO, functional leads]  
**Purpose:** Focus discussion on decisions, blockers, risks, ownership, and follow-through.

| Time | Topic | Owner | Outcome needed |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 0-5 | Open, objectives, changes since last meeting | PMO | Align on meeting purpose |
| 5-15 | Exceptions and top portfolio signals | PMO | Confirm focus areas |
| 15-30 | Decisions needed | Sponsors / owners | Decide or define missing inputs |
| 30-45 | Blockers, dependencies, escalations | Owners | Confirm next action |
| 45-55 | Actions and follow-ups | PMO | Confirm owners and dates |
| 55-60 | Closeout | PMO | Confirm carry-forward items |
