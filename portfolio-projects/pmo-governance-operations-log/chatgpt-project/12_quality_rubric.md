# Quality Rubric

Governance usefulness:
- Does the output separate status from decisions?
- Are blockers and escalations visible?
- Are owners and due dates clear?
- Are unresolved questions called out?
- Are next steps practical?

Signal quality:
- Does the update say what changed?
- Is evidence provided?
- Is the status current?
- Is the risk or dependency actionable?
- Is "green" status supported?

Human-control quality:
- Does the output avoid making decisions?
- Does it preserve sponsor and stakeholder accountability?
- Does it clearly frame recommendations as advisory?
