# Weekly Governance Summary Prompt

Review the PMO operations log for the cycle and produce a weekly governance summary.

Output sections:
1. Executive summary
2. Decisions needed
3. Escalation candidates
4. Overdue or blocked actions
5. Stakeholder follow-ups
6. Risks, issues, and dependencies
7. Weak or stale status signals
8. Project plan update recommendations
9. Items to carry into the next governance meeting
10. Clarification questions

Keep the summary decision-oriented and operational.
