# Common Defects and Remedies

| Defect | Why it matters | Remedy |
|---|---|---|
| Sponsor not named | No authority to approve scope, funding, or tradeoffs | Name sponsor or approval body before finalizing |
| Business owner missing | No accountable owner for outcomes | Name post-delivery owner or operational accountable party |
| Scope is thematic | Team cannot plan or control change | Define systems, processes, teams, phases, and exclusions |
| Objectives are deliverables | Success becomes activity-based | Convert deliverables into outcome measures |
| Success metrics are vague | Sponsor cannot judge success | Add measurable targets or baseline plan |
| Risks are generic | Risk register will be performative | Name project-specific risks, owners, and mitigation direction |
| Dependencies lack owners | Delays become surprises | Identify dependency owners or mark as open decision |
| Governance is ceremonial | Meetings occur without decisions | Define cadence, decisions, thresholds, and escalation path |
