# Scoring Model Completeness Rubric

| Dimension | Ready | Partial | Not ready |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Criteria clarity | Criteria are distinct, plain-language, and relevant. | Some criteria overlap or need clearer anchors. | Criteria are vague, duplicative, or misaligned. |
| Weight balance | Weights total 100% and reflect strategy. | Weights total 100% but rationale is weak. | Weights do not total 100% or are arbitrary. |
| Scale definitions | 1-5 anchors are defined and usable. | Anchors exist but are uneven. | No usable scale definitions. |
| Mandatory-work treatment | Mandatory work is explicitly classified and separated. | Mandatory work is noted but not operationalized. | Mandatory work is hidden inside ranking. |
| Evidence standard | Scores require evidence or assumption notes. | Evidence notes are inconsistent. | Scores lack support. |
| Confidence rating | Each item has High, Medium, or Low confidence. | Confidence is applied inconsistently. | No confidence rating. |
| Governance fit | Decision rights and board cadence are explicit. | Governance exists but ownership is unclear. | No clear decision authority. |
| Auditability | Logs and score rationale can be reviewed. | Some rationale exists. | No audit trail. |
